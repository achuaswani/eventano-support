Getting Started

  • Sign In: Tap “Continue” to sign in securely.
  • Create Profile: Add your first and last name.
  • Register Face: Capture a few selfies so we can find your photos in events.
  • Subscribe to Events: Enter an Event ID or scan a QR code.

Common Questions

  • No photos in My Feed: Make sure you’ve registered your face and joined events.
  • How to share/unshare: Tap the share button on a photo to toggle its status.
  • Delete face captures: Go to Capture Face and delete selected faces.
  • Delete photos: Use in‑app delete where available or email us for assistance.
